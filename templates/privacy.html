Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account details: your name and email address provided during registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, actions taken).

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports.
  • To understand how the app is used and improve its features.

We will never sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — used to load the app's typefaces. Google may log font requests.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms that track you across sites, or any service that receives your expense data.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains no personal information and is deleted when you sign out or close your browser session.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We will respond as promptly as we can.
